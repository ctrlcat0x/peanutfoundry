---
title: Contributing to Peanut Foundry
---

We welcome contributions from everyone!  
> Help us make Peanut Foundry better by sharing your knowledge, improving guides, or suggesting new tools.

## How to Contribute

1. **Fork the repository** on GitHub.
2. **Create a new branch** for your changes.
3. **Make your edits** (guides, tools, corrections, etc).
4. **Submit a pull request** with a clear description.

## What Can You Contribute?

- New guides or tutorials
- Corrections or improvements to existing content
- Useful tools or resources
- UI/UX suggestions and bug reports

## Community Guidelines

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Cite sources where appropriate.
- Keep content clear, concise, and accessible.

> **Thank you for helping build a better knowledge base!**
