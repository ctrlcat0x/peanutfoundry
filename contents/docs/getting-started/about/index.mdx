Welcome to **Peanut Foundry**!  
Peanut Foundry is an open-source knowledge base and toolkit for power users, tinkerers, and curious minds.

## What is Peanut Foundry?

Peanut Foundry is a curated collection of guides, tools, and resources designed to help you get the most out of your software and devices.  
Whether you’re a beginner or an expert, you’ll find something useful here.

## Features

- 📚 In-depth guides for all skill levels
- 🛠️ Curated tools and utilities
- 🧑‍💻 Community-driven contributions
- 🌗 Modern, accessible, and beautiful UI

## Our Mission

To empower users with clear, practical, and trustworthy information—making technology more accessible for everyone.
