# Welcome to **Peanut Foundry**

Peanut Foundry is an open-source knowledge base and toolkit for power users, tinkerers, and curious minds. Our mission is to empower you with clear, practical, and trustworthy information—making technology more accessible for everyone.

---

## What is Peanut Foundry?

Peanut Foundry is a curated collection of guides, tools, and resources designed to help you get the most out of your software and devices. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, you'll find something useful here.

## Our Mission
